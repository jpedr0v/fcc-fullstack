Omelette recipe

A quick, delicious and nutritious meal. Made with eggs!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 slice of ham
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • green seasoning to taste
  • chicken stock to taste

Instructions

  1. Beat the 2 eggs, you can use a mixer or not.
  2. After you've beaten it well, put it in the oiled frying pan, add the salt, the diced ham and the two slices of cheese (you don't need to chop the cheese).
  3. Add seasoning to taste, wait for it to firm up and turn the omelette over.
  4. A delicious omelette is ready, enjoy!
